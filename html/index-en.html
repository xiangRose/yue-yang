About Yueyang

Dongguan Yueyang Wire & Cable Co., Ltd. was founded in February 2002, headquartered in Humen, Dongguan, Guangdong, China. We are a professional wire and cable manufacturer integrating R&D, production, sales, and service.

We are committed to producing high-quality cables across all series and sizes. Our products are widely used in industrial equipment, mobile communications, energy & power, security systems, medical devices, and automotive transportation.
