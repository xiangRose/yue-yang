About Yueyang

Dongguan Yueyang Wire & Cable Co., Ltd. is a leading private enterprise integrating R&D, production, sales, and service. Since our establishment in 2002, we have continuously expanded production capacity. Today we operate 5 manufacturing bases in China (Dongguan, Sichuan) and Thailand, plus a service center in the United States, serving customers worldwide.

With the vision of "being a trusted global leader in wire and cable manufacturing", we adhere to innovation, quality, and sustainability. We provide wire and interconnect solutions for medical, automotive, security, gaming, and new energy industries.
